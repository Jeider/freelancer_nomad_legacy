Help: [b]Bases with vulnerable parts[/b]

[b]Short info[/b]
This is bases combined with 3 types of elements:
1. Outer parts
2. Core
3. Vulnerable parts.

[b]How to destroy[/b]
You can pass the core by closed tunnels.
You need attack vulnerable parts to get this pass to core.
Vulnerable parts have different color on your interface.
Attack the core to destroy this base.

[b]Where I can find bases like that[/b]
Missions in bar.

[b]What a profit of this missions[/b]
Additonal commodities and reward, dropped after destruction of this base.
